About Momotaro Nashville

Traditional Technique & Contemporary Elegance

Momotaro arrives in Nashville's Wedgewood Houston neighborhood, carrying forward the philosophy that has defined its celebrated Chicago home — tightly executed cuisine, deeply personal service, and an unwavering respect for Japanese culinary tradition.

Led by Chef Partner Gene Kato and the Boka Restaurant Group, Momotaro Nashville is a new chapter in a story that has always been rooted in craft — a modern dining experience shaped by the seasons, the land, and the enduring elegance of Japanese cooking.

Located at 519 Houston Street in the heart of Wedgewood Houston, Momotaro is Nashville's destination for sushi, sashimi, robata-grilled dishes, and handcrafted Japanese cocktails. The menu changes with the seasons, drawing on the finest domestic and imported ingredients. Reservations are available nightly, Tuesday through Saturday, via OpenTable.
